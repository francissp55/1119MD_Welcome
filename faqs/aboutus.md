# The owners

Hello, we are Bea and Steve Francis. We bought 1119 Maryland in 2017.

Bea was born and raised in New Jersey and after college, became an elementary teacher. Bea's first teaching job was at Eielson AFB, Alaska, teaching for the Fairbanks North Star School District.
Steve was born and raised in England. He moved to the US in 1967 when he was 12. Steve moved around a lot as his stepfather was in the USAF. After graduating from college, Steve joined the USAF flying as a navigator KC-135s and RC-135s in Washington and Alaska
which is where they both met.

Steve left the AF and became a CPA at Price Waterhouse in Anchorage, AK. Our first child, Julie was born in Fairbanks, AK. We moved to Anchorage, then to Portland, OR when our second child, John was born. Eventually, Steve's job took us to New Jersey. 

Bea remembered spending time in Cape May when she was younger. After we moved to NJ we started going to Cape May with our children. We had always thought about buying a place and might have done sooner but for Steve accepting a job transfer to London. We spent 8 years in London but maintained our home in Princeton. 
Our children grew up and while we were still in London, our first grandchild was born. Not long after moving back, Steve decided to retire from 18 years at General Electric. We started going back to Cape May with an expanded family and eventually purchased 1119 Maryland. We get to use it in the quieter season and the grandchildren love coming to Cape May.

We have made quite a few changes to the house since 2017. We are already on our 3rd Refrigerator!. All but one of the porch areas have been screened in.
A new shed was put up early on. We have renovated 2 of the bathrooms, replaced every appliance, every bed, every TV, most of the furniture and even put a sliding door from the kitchen to the porch.
