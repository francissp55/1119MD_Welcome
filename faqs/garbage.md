# Garbage and Recycling

Garbage and Recyling goes out Wednesday evening. Good News! We have a service that will take the garbage and recylcing
to the curb and back! If It is not put out, contact (text or voice) Cape May Garbage Service at 609 970 7095

Please follow the rule for recycling. The township will remove stuff they do not recycle.

Please get as much out the day of collection. Too often the trash and recycling cans are not filled until checkout day
which means there is no capacity for the incoming renters until Thursday.

Crush cans, boxes etc. Do not put food scraps in recycling.
