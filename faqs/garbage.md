

# Garbage and Recycling

Garbage and Recyling goes out Wednesday evening. Good News! We have a service that will take the garbage and recylcing
to the curb and back! If It is not put out, contact (text or voice) Shoreline Waste Solution at 609 970 7095

Please follow the rule for recycling. The township will remove stuff they do not recycle.

Please put as much out the day of collection(Wedneday). Do not leave the trash and recycling cans so full on Saturday the incoming renters have no space in the cans.

Crush cans, boxes etc. Do not put food scraps in recycling.
