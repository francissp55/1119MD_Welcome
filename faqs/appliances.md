# Washer & Dryer
- Washer. The washer doesn't like sand. On average we are having to replace the washer every 5 years. 
- Clean lint trap before each use. The vent for the exhaust is on the second floor porch. It is cleaned at the start
  of the season. A mesh bag catches the lint but the bag may need to be emptied.

# Dishwasher
- The Bosch dishwasher has a cutlery tray that you may not be aware of. It is in the top of the dishwasher.
- Use provided detergent pods. 
- Normal cycle is usually sufficient.
- Be sure to run the night before checkout. The wash cycle is quite long.
- Empty the dishwasher.

# Refrigerator
- It has an icemaker but no residential refrigerator is designed to provide unlimited ice for 15 people.
- It can also produce ice in a second container in the freezer.
- Clean up spills and empty the refrigerator.

# Keurig
- Remove used pods when you leave. The coffee make will be descaled before the season starts. A regular drip coffee maker
  is available.
