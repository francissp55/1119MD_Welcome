# Wi‑Fi Information

The network name and password is provided separately

The Internet provider is XFINITY. We have a 500MBPS service.

Other features

- We have a nighthawk wifi mesh system. You will see black boxes on all floors. Please do not move them
- We have moca adapters and some devices, such as the Living Room TV have a wired ethernet connection
- The 3rd floor twin room with desk has an ethernet bridge.

If you experience issues:
- Try toggling Wi‑Fi on your device
- Restart the router (power off 10 seconds, then on). Restarting the router takes as long as 12 minutes
