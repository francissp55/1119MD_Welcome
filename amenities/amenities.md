
# Beachbox
The beach box is on the Reading Beach access. We moved from the Philadelphia access since that area gets more crowded, especially with hotels hogging the space. Bring your own lock.

# Beachtags
Six commercial beach tags with lanyards are provided. Please return them. If you are a veteran and need another beach tag they are available for free.

# Propane Gas
We provide two full propane tanks at the start of the season. There is an honor system. If you use up a tank, please take the empty to Swains Hardware for a swap. You can go to Lowes but that is an extra 30 minute roundtrip.

# Games/Videos/Books
They are for the enjoyment of all. Please understand that if a game piece is missing, that is life. We have cornhole in the shed. Do not leave it out overnight or in the rain. The sprinkler system comes on at night and the equipment is not weatherproof.

# Youtube TV
Is installed so you can watch TV channels. The smart tvs have a variety of streaming apps. I recommend logging out when you leave, especially is you can purchase content.

# Sonos
If you know how to log in to Sonos you can. If you own a Sonos system you know it is notoriously fickle for staying connected
often requiring a factory reset.
