# Beachbox
The beach box is on the Reading Beach access. We moved from the Philadelphia access since that area gets more crowded, especially with hotels hogging the space.

# Beachtags
Six commercial beach tags with lanyards are provided. Please return them.

# Propane Gas
We provide two propane tanks at the start of the season. There is an honor system. If you use up a tank, please take the empty to Swains Hardware for a swap. You can go to Lowes but that is an extra 30 minute trip

# Games/Videos/Books
They are for the enjoyment of all. Please understand that is a game piece is missing, that is life. We have cornhole in the shed. Do not leave it out overnight or in the rain. The sprinkler system comes on at night and the equipment is not weatherproof.
