# TV Guide

If you brought your own streaming device, please remember to take it with you.

If you have signed into any streaming apps from the TV, remember to sign out.

If the TV does not show the welcome screen:

1. Make sure the HDMI stick or input is powered
2. Confirm the TV is on the correct input
3. Restart the device by unplugging for 10 seconds

If issues persist, contact us through the rental platform.
